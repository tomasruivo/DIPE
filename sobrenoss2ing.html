About Us

Dissemination of ideas for companies

The main objective of this site is to provide innovative ideas for products and projects to be developed or that have already been implemented to companies looking for this type of information. One of the advantages will be simple and quick access, benefiting young people or adults, who wish to benefit from sponsorship from a given company, in order to improve their project or place it in the job market.

Where did we come from and what are we

Our emergence started when we realized that young people don't have a way to publish innovative ideas in a simple and practical way or even print doesn't have a practical way to find new ideas, so with that in mind Dipe emerged, we will serve as a point that will connect ideas to the press always in the most practical and simple way possible because we believe that the innovative ideas of young people are what prove that we have a future full of new possibilities.

I want to thank everyone who participates or contributes to this campaign, as it helps us to continue and give futures to young people and companies in need.
